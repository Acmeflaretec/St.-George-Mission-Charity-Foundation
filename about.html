about us

St. George Mission Charity Foundation is an NGO based in Angamaly, Kerala, India. Since our establishment in 2015, we have been committed to individual empowerment, family welfare, and community development.

Vision:

Our vision is to creatively intervene for social transformation through effective empowerment and productive compassion.

Mission:

We empower individuals, families, and social groups to lead happier lives with healthier relationships. We serve less privileged fellow beings with love by fostering intrapersonal and interpersonal harmony.

Our services include palliative home care, financial assistance for treatments, education support, community outreach, counseling by psychologists and psychiatrists, free medical camps, and corporate training programs.

Our mission

St. George Mission Charity Foundation is to empower individuals, families, and social groups to lead happier lives with healthier relationships. We strive to serve less privileged fellow beings with love by fostering intrapersonal and interpersonal harmony. Through our initiatives in individual empowerment, family welfare, and community development, we aim to create lasting positive change.

We provide palliative home care services, financial assistance for treatments, education support for eligible students, and community outreach programs benefiting tribal colonies. Our counseling services by psychologists and psychiatrists offer vital guidance. We conduct free medical camps, blood donation drives, and corporate training programs to enhance well-being and empower communities. Together, we build a compassionate, thriving society.
